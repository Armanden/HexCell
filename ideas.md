# new game jam on raylb


# the constraints:
- 64 mb
- wasm
- res 720x720

# theme:
- hex+merge

# initial idea:
have drafting hexagons in space or a cell
and these hexagons can merge and they have different abilities, they add on to each other and become larger, the player controlls the main hexagon
but some hexagons arent complient with others contact with these hexaons damages the player.

# objective of the game:
to reach cell state(where the player has a solid molecule of minimum 16 hexagons).

make this you have 4-5 days.
